About Sonlight Services

We help homeowners in Traverse City and surrounding towns keep chimney and vent systems clean, documented, and safer to operate year-round.

More Detailed Service Support for Traverse City Homes

Sonlight Services focuses on chimney and flue system condition, cleaning quality, and practical fire-safety recommendations. We work with woodstoves, gas fireplaces, and pellet stoves across Traverse City and nearby service areas.

Every visit can include cleaning, inspection notes, and recommendations based on what we actually find. When needed, we provide Level 1 and Level 2 video inspections to show internal conditions clearly.

Beyond chimney systems, we also provide dryer vent cleaning and gutter cleaning to reduce buildup and improve overall household safety and maintenance performance.

Inspection Quality

Level 1 and Level 2 video inspection options to better verify flue and chimney condition.

Minor Repairs

Rain caps, fire bricks, baffle boards, rope gaskets, crack repairs, and flashing reseal.

Safety Standard

All work done to NFPA 211 standards. No masonry work or installation of flue liners.
